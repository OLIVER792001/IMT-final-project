## üìñ Project Overview

**5000 Years of Chinese Porcelain** is a "Multimedia Museum Meta Mirror" (MMMM) project representing a digital exhibition of Chinese ceramics history. 

It was created for the course **Information Modelling and Web Technologies** for the **2024/2025** academic year, with **Professor Fabio Vitali**, for the Master's degree in **Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge (DHDK)** at the **University of Bologna**.

Our goal is to present the evolution of this art form through an interactive interface, allowing users to explore high-quality images and detailed narratives about specific artifacts based on the MMMM framework.
